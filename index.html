About

With over two decades of web development expertise, my journey began in the early days of the internet, hacking away at my first website on in 1997.

By 1999, I was working full-time on dynamic web applications using (classic) ASP, laying the groundwork for a long career in tech. In 2006, I transitioned to WordPress, harnessing its power to create dynamic, user-friendly sites.

My passion for innovation led me to embrace modern JavaScript frameworks, and since 2019, I've been thriving as a full-stack developer, specializing in React and Next.js to build cutting-edge web applications.
